Biography

Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Jobs was also the largest shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios and founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s, alongside Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak. "Creative entrepreneur" was a label often associated with him, as his passion for perfection and ferocious drive revolutionized various industries: personal computers, animated movies, music, phones, tablets, computing, and digital publishing.
